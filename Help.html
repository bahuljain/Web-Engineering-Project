FAQ

› What is Food Express all about?

Food Express provides an easy way to get your favourite cuisine delivered at your doorstep.


› How does Food Express work?

Register and Select a specific branch of Food Express closer to your place.Click on order.Select your favourite cuisine.Once you have placed an order select mode of payment.Food Express does the rest of the work!


› Can we customize our order not strictly based on the menu?

Unfortunately,you are not able to customize your order.


› How do i know if my order got through?

You will recieve a confirmation email form us,stating the orders that you have selected and the time it will take to be delivered to you.


› How do i sign up for Food Express?

Click on the login tab which is located at the top right hand side of the home page.Fill in your details and click submit.You will receive a confirmation shortly.


› How do i cancel my order?

Give us a call at 011 3071 5375 (11 Am to 11 PM).


› I did not receive what i ordered . Why did this happen?

There may be times when a mistake has been made and items from your order are missing when the delivery is made.You can contact us and and we can find a replacement for the same.


› How long does te delivery take?

Depends on the number of orders and how far is the restaurant from delivery address.


› Are there any discounts?

There are discounts for members depending on how frequently you have been ordering from Food Express.You will recieve a email form us for the same.


› How do i provide feedback?

You can call us on our customer care 011 3071 5375 (11 Am to 11 PM) or send us a mail at contact@foodexpress.in
